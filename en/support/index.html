FAQ

Common questions

The app says data is not downloaded. What should I try?

Open the app with a stable network connection and pull to refresh on the Home screen. The app downloads a manifest first, then verifies the market data files before showing them.

Are prices guaranteed?

No. Prices are estimates intended for comparison while shopping. Stall prices can vary by quality, neighborhood, weather, timing, and seller.

Does the app need an account?

No. Saved items, basket items, filters, and settings live on your device. Optional bought-price feedback uses a random contributor ID instead of an account login.

Where is the privacy policy?

The privacy policy is available at twbraam.github.io/miaachaitaiwan/en/privacy/.
